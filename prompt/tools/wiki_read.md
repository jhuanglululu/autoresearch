Read a summary from the shared research wiki by slug. Read-only.

The output ends with an evidence footer: which sources it cites (fetched URLs and
experiment runs are re-verifiable; captures are testimony), whether any cited source was
retracted, its typed relations, and which notes reference it.

Only sources are evidence. A summary citing another summary is navigation, not support — a
claim repeated across many summaries is no better verified than when it was first written.
When weighing a claim, follow the chain down to sources, and prefer fetched URLs
(re-checkable) and experiment runs (reproducible) over plain captures (another agent's
testimony).

Slug format: lowercase with hyphens, no spaces (e.g. "rope-scaling"). Use `wiki_search`
first when you don't know the exact slug.
